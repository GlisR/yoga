Recommended Books

Deepen your yoga practice with these inspiring reads

Light on Yoga

Author: B.K.S. Iyengar

The definitive guide to yoga practice, featuring detailed instructions and photographs of over 200 postures. This classic text is essential for both beginners and advanced practitioners seeking to deepen their understanding of yoga asanas and philosophy.

The Yoga Sutras of Patanjali

Author: Sri Swami Satchidananda

An accessible translation and commentary on the ancient wisdom of Patanjali's Yoga Sutras. This book provides profound insights into the philosophy and practice of yoga, offering guidance for living a meaningful and peaceful life.

The Heart of Yoga

Author: T.K.V. Desikachar

A comprehensive guide to yoga practice that emphasizes adapting yoga to individual needs. This book covers asana, pranayama, and meditation with clear explanations and practical advice for developing a personal practice.

Autobiography of a Yogi

Author: Paramahansa Yogananda

A spiritual classic that has inspired millions worldwide. This autobiography shares the life story of one of the most beloved spiritual teachers, offering profound insights into yoga, meditation, and the quest for self-realization.
